Mini Gherkins on Toothpicks

Ingredients

  • 1 14 ounces jar Mini Gherkins (cornichons)
  • 1 small box of toothpicks

Directions

  1. 1. Drain pickles in sink to remove all liquid
  2. 2. Arrange pickles on platter and stick with toothpicks
