Magic Mushroom Mini Omelets

Ingredients

  • half white or yellow onion, diced
  • 1 pound cremini mushrooms, diced
  • 1 cup leftover brown rice
  • 1 dozen eggs
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • half bunch scallions, sliced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. 1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. 2. Grease mini-muffin tin (24 cups) with sesame oil and set aside.
  3. 3. In a skillet, melt butter and saute garlic and onion until soft, then add mushrooms until brown.
  4. 4. Crack 1 dozen eggs into a bowl and whisk to combine. Fold in rice, scallions, and onion and mushroom mixture. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. 5. Spoon mixture into greased mini-muffin tin and cook for 8-10 minutes.
  6. 6. Serve hot out of the oven or at room temperature with hoisin, sriracha, and soy sauce.
